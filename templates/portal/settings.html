Demand Thresholds (Working Days)

Defines the day thresholds for Large / Medium / Small demand buckets. Demand = total working days of a discipline's tasks overlapping the next 60 days. Default: Large > 16d, Medium 5–16d, Small < 5d (matching ≈120hr / 40hr at 7.5hr/day).
